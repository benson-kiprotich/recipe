Perfect Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a sufuria, combine the rice, water, and salt.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and water is absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
